# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 6, 2026

Celebray ("we", "our", or "the app") is a celebration reminder app developed by Shegz. This policy explains how we handle your information.

## Information We Collect

- **Account information:** If you sign in with Google or Apple, we store your name, email, and profile photo locally on your device using secure storage.
- **Event data:** Celebrations you add (names, dates, relationships, memories) are stored locally on your device in a SQLite database.
- **Crash reports:** Anonymous crash data may be collected via Firebase Crashlytics to improve app stability.

## How We Use Information

- To provide reminder notifications for your celebrations
- To personalize message generation within the app
- To authenticate your account when you choose to sign in

## Data Storage

All event data is stored locally on your device. We do not sync your celebrations to a cloud server in the current version.

## Third-Party Services

- **Firebase Authentication** — sign-in with Google and Apple
- **Firebase Crashlytics** — crash reporting
- **Google Sign-In / Sign in with Apple** — authentication

## Your Rights

You can delete your account and local profile data from Settings. You can delete individual events from the Reminders screen. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact: privacy@celebray.app
