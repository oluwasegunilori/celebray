Common questions

How do I enable reminders?

Allow notification permissions when prompted. Celebray sends a 7-day advance reminder and a notification on the celebration day.

Where is my data stored?

Your celebrations are stored locally on your device. Sign-in is optional and used for account features only.

How do I delete my account?

Open Settings in the app and tap "Delete Account" to remove your profile data from the device.

How do I delete the app?

Uninstall Celebray from your device. This removes all locally stored celebration data.
